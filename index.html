Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

Since 2020 Founded by Mr. Govind Shukla

InAmigos Foundation is a Central Government licensed Section 8 non-profit organization headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. What began on September 23, 2020 as a small community effort by founder Mr. Govind Shukla has grown into a nationwide movement touching over 50,000 lives across 28 states.

We are driven by a singular belief: every individual, regardless of their background, deserves access to education, nourishment, dignity, and a healthy environment. Our six flagship initiatives — BachpanShala, Seva, Udaan, Jeev, Prakriti, and Vikas — span education, food security, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental conservation, and skill development.

Our Mission

To serve humanity with compassion by empowering underprivileged communities through education, welfare, and sustainable development — one village, one child, one life at a time.

Our Vision

A nation where every child has access to quality education, every woman is empowered, every animal is protected, and every community thrives in harmony with nature.

What We Do

Our Flagship Initiatives

Six interconnected projects creating lasting change across India — each with its own identity, mission, and community of dedicated volunteers.

Education

BachpanShala

“Nurturing Young Minds, Building Bright Futures”

Providing free quality education to underprivileged children across rural and urban India. BachpanShala runs community learning centres where volunteer educators teach foundational literacy, numeracy, and essential life skills to children who lack access to formal schooling. The initiative focuses on building confidence and curiosity in every child, one classroom at a time.

Welfare

Seva

“Serving Humanity with Compassion”

Organising regular food distribution and clothing drives for homeless and underprivileged families across India. Seva ensures that no one within our reach goes hungry or without basic necessities — especially during harsh winters, festivals, and natural disasters. Volunteers mobilise community donations and coordinate ground-level distribution with dignity and care.

Empowerment

Udaan

“Soaring Towards a Brighter Future”

Empowering women through skill development workshops, vocational training, and awareness campaigns. Udaan equips women with practical skills and confidence to become financially independent leaders in their communities. From tailoring and handicrafts to digital literacy and entrepreneurship, the programme creates pathways to self-reliance.

Animal Welfare

Jeev

“Empowering Lives, Spreading Compassion”

Dedicated to rescuing, feeding, and protecting stray and injured animals across Indian cities and villages. Jeev coordinates veterinary care for injured strays, runs regular feeding drives, and conducts community awareness campaigns on animal welfare, anti-cruelty laws, and compassionate coexistence with all living beings.

Environment

Prakriti

“Plant for a Better Tomorrow”

Championing environmental sustainability through large-scale tree plantation drives, neighbourhood clean-up campaigns, and eco-awareness workshops in schools and colleges. Prakriti is our commitment to fighting climate change at the grassroots level — engaging youth and communities to take ownership of their local ecosystems.

Skill Building

Vikas

“Step In, Stand Out, Create Impact”

Bridging the gap between education and employment by providing youth with industry-relevant skills, mentorship, and career guidance. Vikas prepares young individuals from underserved backgrounds to become self-reliant through resume workshops, interview training, digital skills, and connections to employment opportunities.

The Impact We’ve Made

From a single initiative in Chhattisgarh to a nationwide movement — here’s our journey in numbers and milestones.

2020

The Beginning

InAmigos Foundation is established on September 23 in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh by Mr. Govind Shukla with a small group of passionate volunteers.

2021

First Initiatives Launch

BachpanShala and Seva are launched, bringing free education and food distribution to underprivileged communities in Chhattisgarh.

2022

Expanding Horizons

Udaan, Jeev, and Prakriti join the initiative portfolio. The volunteer network expands beyond Chhattisgarh to neighbouring states.

2023

Government Recognition

InAmigos receives 80G, 12A, CSR-1 certifications and NITI Aayog registration, enabling tax-exempt donations and corporate CSR partnerships.

2024–25

Nationwide & Quality Certified

Operations reach all 28 states with 200+ active volunteers. IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification confirms international quality standards in all operations.
